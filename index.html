About InAmigos Foundation

InAmigos Foundation is a Section 8 registered NGO working towards education, women empowerment, food distribution, animal welfare, and environmental sustainability.

The organization conducts social campaigns, volunteer programs, and awareness initiatives across India.

Our Projects

Project SEVA

Food distribution drives and support for underprivileged communities.

Project BACHPANSHALA

Supporting children's education and learning opportunities.

Project JEEV

Animal welfare and feeding drives for stray animals.

Project PRAKRITI

Tree plantation and environmental awareness campaigns.

Project UDAAN

Empowering women through skill development, education, and career opportunities.

Project VIKAS

Helping youth gain practical experience through internships and skill-building programs.
